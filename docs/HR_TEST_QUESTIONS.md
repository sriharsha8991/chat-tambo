# HR Test Questions (7)

- [ ] Can HR post a new announcement and see it appear for employees and managers?
- [ ] Can HR upload a PDF document and see it listed in the HR Document Center?
- [ ] Can employees and managers open the uploaded PDF from their dashboards?
- [ ] Can HR set a document to require acknowledgment and see acknowledgments recorded?
- [ ] Can HR create a new policy and see it in the Policy Manager and Policy Viewer?
- [ ] Can HR edit an existing policy and see updates reflected immediately?
- [ ] Can HR delete a policy and confirm it no longer appears in the Policy Viewer?
